About Me

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineering student at the University of Derby, specializing in analog integrated circuit (IC) design. My interests include low-noise amplifier design, power management circuits, and analog layout techniques.

I have hands-on experience with circuit simulation, PCB design, and prototyping, and I am actively building a portfolio of analog and mixed-signal projects. My goal is to contribute to the development of high-performance, reliable analog ICs for applications in sensing, communications, and power electronics.

I am seeking opportunities to further develop my skills in analog IC design through internships, graduate programs, or entry-level roles in the semiconductor industry.
